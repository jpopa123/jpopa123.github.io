Idaho is a playground for anyone who loves the outdoors. From hiking and biking in the mountains to kayaking and fishing in crystal-clear lakes, there’s always something to keep you active. The state’s diverse landscapes, from volcanic lava fields to dense forests, make every adventure unique and memorable. Whether you’re chasing thrills or just want to relax in nature, Idaho has the perfect activity for you.

Beyond outdoor adventures, Idaho also offers cultural and family-friendly experiences. You can explore historic towns, visit local farms, or attend seasonal festivals and events that celebrate the state’s heritage. Every season brings new opportunities for fun, from skiing in the winter to boating in the summer, making Idaho an ideal destination year-round.

Popular Idaho Activies:

  • Hiking & Backpacking – Sawtooth Mountains, Hells Canyon, and Boise Foothills
  • Skiing & Snowboarding – Sun Valley, Bogus Basin
  • Water Sports – Kayaking on the Snake River, boating on Lake Coeur d’Alene
  • Fishing – Salmon River, Payette Lake
  • Camping – Craters of the Moon, Priest Lake, Payette National Forest
  • Cultural Experiences – Old Idaho Penitentiary, Basque Block in Boise
  • Festivals & Events – Treefort Music Fest (Boise), McCall Winter Carnival
