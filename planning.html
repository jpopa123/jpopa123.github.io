Visiting Idaho is easy when you plan ahead. Knowing the best time to travel, how to get around, and where to stay can make your trip much more enjoyable. A little planning goes a long way toward a stress-free visit.

Idaho offers options for every type of traveler — from cozy cabins and lakeside resorts to city hotels and campgrounds. Planning routes, checking local events, and packing for changing weather will help you enjoy every hike, festival, and scenic view without rushing.

Planning Tips & Resources

  • Best Time to Visit – Summer for lakes & hiking, winter for skiing
  • Getting Around – Rent a car; public transport is limited
  • Where to Stay – Boise, Sun Valley, McCall, Coeur d’Alene, campgrounds
  • Packing Tips – Layers, hiking shoes, sunscreen, water bottles
  • Local Events – Festivals, farmer’s markets, seasonal activities
  • Maps & Guides – Idaho Travel Bureau, visitor centers
