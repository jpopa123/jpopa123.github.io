Idaho is packed with destinations that balance wild scenery, small-town charm, and outdoor adventure. From dramatic mountain ranges to quiet lakes and historic towns, the state offers places that feel untouched while still being welcoming and easy to explore.

Whether you’re looking to hike, relax, ski, or just take in the views, Idaho has something for every type of traveler. Each destination highlights a different side of the state, nature, history, or culture, making it perfect for road trips or longer stays.

Popular Idaho Destinations & Things To Do:

  • Boise – Explore the Greenbelt, downtown food scene, and nearby foothills
  • Sun Valley – Skiing, hiking, and a classic mountain resort vibe
  • Coeur d’Alene – Lake activities, beaches, and scenic boat cruises
  • Shoshone Falls – Massive waterfalls often called the “Niagara of the West”
  • Craters of the Moon – Unique volcanic landscapes and lava fields
  • Sawtooth Mountains – Alpine lakes, camping, and backpacking
  • McCall – Cozy lake town with year-round outdoor recreation
